# Autonomy in Agentic AI

## Definition
Autonomy means the ability of an agent to **act independently**, without constant human intervention.

## Key Points
- Agents can initiate tasks on their own.
- They adapt dynamically to changing inputs or environments.
- Autonomy differentiates agents from passive ML models.

## Example
A customer support agent that not only answers queries but also **escalates issues** or **creates tickets** automatically.

## Interview Tip
Highlight autonomy as the **core differentiator** between agentic systems and traditional ML pipelines.
